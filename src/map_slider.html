Albuquerque, New Mexico from 1990 to 2020

View historical imagery of Albuquerque as well as changes in population, median household income, and Rio Grande discharge rates.

How to use this map

Click and drag in the map to pan your view. Use the "+" and "-" buttons to zoom the view in or out.
Use the slider on the bottom of the map to change the year of displayed imagery and data.
